1. Senior software developer

At Plus500crypto we are on a mission to establish the topmost innovative and industry-leading cryptocurrencies exchange. our team is comprised of many energetic young individuals that are self-driven and motivated towards innovation and execution of lots of ideas and tasks with speed and accuracy.

Responsibilities

As an Engineer with Plus500crypto you’ll be a principal member of the team that builds the Plus500crypto core platform: that is, the trading engine, data analysis algorithms, API, wallet management, 3rd party integrations, etc.

1. The Architecture: Plus500crypto backend is built with the micro-services architecture in mind. Redundancy and high availability are main factors and lead every choice made to design each piece on the platform.

2. Trading engine: best in class trading engine across all trading and crypto exchanges. Contribute to design, new order types development, performance tuning and research.

3. Data analysis algorithms: the data we present to our users is the result of tailored algorithms on top of the incredible amount of data our platform produces daily.

4. APIs: the public face the presents the complexity of our architecture in a clean interface. Design, develop and improve trading and account management APIs on top of Web Sockets, REST and custom high-performance protocol.

Requirements

1. Self-motivated and Responsible. No one on the team will micro-manage you. We are looking for team members who are comfortable taking an initiative

2. Knowledge and experience in micro-services architecture

3. Comfortable with high-availability concepts

4. Have actively participated in the development of a sophisticated platform as one of the leading developers

5. Extreme dedication to work

6. Ability to quickly learn new technologies

7. Preferred technologies: Ruby, Node.js, MySQL, Redis, Websockets

8. Other technologies of interest: Message queues (ZeroMQ, RabbitMQ), Job Queues
