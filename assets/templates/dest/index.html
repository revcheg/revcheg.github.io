About Us

Y2P Technologies is a service provider in merchant payments and foreign exchange services for WeChat Pay outside of China.

Y2P was established in 2015 as a Hong Kong Financial Technology gateway for real-time foreign exchange and global funds remittance, with the strategic objective of providing a unique global solution for cross-border payments with China.

Y2P is an innovative independent foreign exchange trading specialist and payment service provider. We help online businesses succeed through cutting-edge technology, world-leading expertise and a culture dedicated to trust.

Y2P has exploited the changing regulatory conditions and gap in servicing payments for inbound and outbound China payments. Our experienced team has extensive knowledge, experience and expertise within FX, coupled with innovative and cutting edge technology. We are successful in applying this to the growing payment demands of users in China in their interface with the rest of the world through WeChat.

Y2P value in FX and payments that connect East and West markets include significant increases in efficiency and transparency, and a corresponding significant reduction in costs and friction in the execution of cross-border monetary transactions.

As an official Institutional Partner of Tencent (WeChat Pay) Y2P has the objective of expanding merchant and in-app payments from China to the rest of the world.

WeChat Pay currently has over 700 million mainland

to Chinese consumers. Usage also allows
