Security

Safety is our topmost Priority

The safty and security of our client's funds and information is something we take really seriously. Our strong security protocols gives our clients to opportunity to secure their accounts

What the imortance of securing you account

as a result of the various transactions made with real money, when you makes a deposit you place your money in a cryptologic account. This is very important that you can control them. If you don't turn on protection feautures in your account, someone can easily get access to the account and make away with your money.

Strong password

it is very important to use a password unique to you alone. This maks it immporsible for anyone to steal your money. The password should contain at least a minimu of 8 characterss and it should be comprised of small letters, big letters, digits and sometimes even special character (!, # &) as the case may be.

Two factor authentication(2FA)

this special feature is available to provide another level of authentication and verification of the right and legal user of an account. With 2FA enabled, you give your account another layer of protection: usually a one time code that can only be sent to your mobile devices.

AML & KYC

Cryotologic exchange is strongly against the act of money laundering and any form of financing terrorism. It consists of

  • verification of your account: before a payment is carried out, any user or customer that wants to pay in fiat currencies has to verify their account
  • Safety PIN code : a 4 digit code is expected to be set up during account registration. If our customer service is contacted for anything, no information would be released without the PIN code
  • Transfer delay or account blooking : we can block, suspend or deactivate an account in the case where by a crime is suspected
  • Singular user, one account : one person is allowed to own only one account on cryptologi
