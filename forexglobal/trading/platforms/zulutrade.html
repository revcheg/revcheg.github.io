ZuluTrade

ZuluTrade is an online trading platform that enables users to locate or subscribe to top performing traders

A trader can execute trades manually or allocate a desirable amount to follow top performing traders, therefore diversifying the investment. The trader can also set risk management parameters in advance of any trading activity, in order to shield against any volatile trading strategy.

ZuluTrade users are able to connect to the platform through the website and the available mobile applications. Innovative Mobile Apps for all major mobile platforms (iPhone, iPad, Android, Blackberry, and Kindle Fire) offer full functionality and the ability to be in control of your account anywhere, anytime.

  • ZuluRank: a state of the art algorithm that ranks signal providers with full transparency

  • Simulation: a powerful tool to simulate the account’s potential performance before going live on real account with a selected strategy, therefore testing various scenarios for minimizing risk
  • Automator: users can create and execute their own automated rules using ‘if this, then that’ logic, with their own custom settings
  • Mobile App: innovative mobile apps help you to stay in control of your account wherever you are
  • Customisation Settings: fully customisable account management suite: set specific instruments, set custom stops and limits, edit or close positions instantly, configure lot sizes and more!

How does it work?

Simply choose from 100,000+ users from 192 countries you want to follow based on a multitude performance factors including stability, exposure and minimum equity required. Check out the Performance Table for live updates!

You then customise the trades executed by the signal provider in accordance with your own preferences and risk management rules. Choose your lot size, set stop-loss parameters and manage the number of trades executed for your preferred currency pair.

There are so many things you can do to customize your individual preferences by choosing among many parameters from the signal provider such as:

  • the stability of the trading activity
  • the performance of the total trading
  • the period of the performance indicated
  • the exposure in number of trades opened at the same time
  • the minimum equity required to follow the specific trader
  • other performance metrics

Additional information

A highly advanced account protection feature that monitors your performance 24/7, and steps in to protect your invested capital whenever needed.

A sophisticated algorithm that ranks Signal Providers by analysing a number of different factors, such as their overall performance (number of pips earned, drawdown, average number of pips per trade etc), stability (whether there are no significant ups and downs in trades), maturity (how long they have been trading for), exposure (how many positions may be opened at the same time), minimum equity required to follow all the trader’s positions, and other performance metrics. All these factors are calculated in a single ranking formula in order to provide forex investors with the easiest way to select the strategy that best suits their trading style.

The Automator is an innovative tool which allows ZuluTrade users to create and execute their own automated rules, using an ‘if this, then that’ logic, and essentially create their own custom settings. The Automator can watch and notify you when things happen on your account or can automatically execute any action you would otherwise perform yourself.
