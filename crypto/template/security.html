Security

Safety is our topmost Priority

The safety and security of our client's funds and information is something we take really seriously. Our strong security protocols give our clients the opportunity to secure their accounts.

What is importance of securing your account

As a result of the various transactions made with real money, when you make a deposit you place your money in a Cryptologi account. This is very important that you can control them. If you don't turn on protection features in your account, someone can easily get access to the account and make away with your money.

Strong password

It is very important to use a password unique to you alone. This makes it impossible for anyone to steal your money. The password should contain at least a minimum of 8 characters and it should be comprised of small letters, big letters, digits and sometimes even special characters (! # &) as the case may be.

Two factor authentication(2FA)

This special feature is available to provide another level of authentication and verification of the right and legal user of an account. With 2FA enabled, you give your account another layer of protection: usually, a one-time code that can only be sent to your mobile devices.

Our site, services, and personel

Every online service and communication occur over secured and trusted channels. The various wallets are stored and kept making us of a PGP encryption. No personal of Cryptologi has direct access to any customer's Bitcoin funds. Likewise, no one will ask you for the password to your personal account. We run an impressive but yet simple financial and security auditing. This gives our customer a guarantee that their funds well safe and accounted for. Every staff member passes a background check, they receive qualitative training and are expected to adhere to each and every of the company's policy.

AML & KYC

Cryptologi exchange is strongly against the act of money laundering and any form of financing terrorism. It consists of:

  • Verification of your account: before a payment is carried out, any user or customer that wants to pay in fiat currencies has to verify their account
  • Safety PIN code: a 4 digit code is expected to be set up during account registration. If our customer service is contacted for anything, no information would be released without the PIN code
  • Transfer delay or account blocking: we can block, suspend or deactivate an account in the case when a crime is suspected
  • Singular user, one account: one person is allowed to own only one account on Cryptologi
